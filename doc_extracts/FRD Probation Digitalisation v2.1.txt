FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (FRD) 
1. Purpose & Scope 
This FRD defines the complete functional specification for the FAITH  Probation Digitalisation project. It 
covers the screens, forms, notifications, letter templates, automations, status flows, reporting module, 
access controls, non-functional requirements, open items, and sign-off structure required for 
implementation and testing. 
The harmonised scope includes new-hire probation (WF1), acting-role probation (WF2), internal FAITH 
e-signature, grade-based probation cycles, extension handling, early confirmation, early termination, 
reporting and analytics, audit logging, and role-based access control.
 
2. System Context & Architecture 
Area Specification 
Host System FAITH  - web-based, role-driven HR platform. 
Authentication FAITH SSO with RBAC roles assigned per user. 
Roles Line Manager, Direct Report, HRBP, People Manager, Rewards, HOD, Senior Leadership / HR 
Director, System, Auditor. 
Notification Engine FAITH internal notification system and email dispatch; recurring daily reminders supported 
by A-03. 
E-Signature Internal FAITH e-signature and acknowledgement through S-10, F-09, and A-10. No third-
party signing integration. 
Data Store Employee Profile Table, probation workflow records, letter records, audit logs, and employee 
document library. 
Audit Log A-09 append-only, read-only audit event logger. 
Employment Status FAITH employment status field updated by A-06 after signing completion where outcome 
requires status update. 
Reporting S-12 uses A-11 to query live probation records and A-09 audit log; no separate reporting 
database required for Phase 2.

5. Workflow & Status Reference 
Status Semantics: Prob-Mth[n]-Review means the Line Manager review is due. When the LM submits F-03, the 
status changes to Prob-Mth[n]-DR-Acpt, meaning the Direct Report acknowledgement window is active. The 
DR acknowledgement or A-02 auto-accept then completes that cycle and routes to the next review trigger or to 
Prob-Pending-Letter after the final cycle.

review model as WF1: E08 and below follows 3 months / 3 review cycles; M09-M12 follows 6 months / 6 
review cycles. Acting allowance remains system-derived and read-only. Acting confirmation uses LT-05; 
acting non-confirmation uses LT-06.
