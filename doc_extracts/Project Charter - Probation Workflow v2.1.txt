PROJECT CHARTER | Probation Process Digitalisation 
PROJECT CHARTER 
Digitalisation of Employee Probation Management Workflow 
 
1. Executive Summary 
This Project Charter formally authorises the Probation Process Digitalisation project within 
the FAITH HRMS platform. The project delivers a fully digitised, workflow-driven probation 
management solution covering automated notifications, structured monthly performance 
reviews, digital letter generation, and real-time employment status updates in FAITH. 
 
Following a decision in May 2026, the project will implement an internal e-signature and 
acknowledgement solution within FAITH rather than integrating with DocuSign. All letter 
signing and acknowledgement workflows will be handled natively within the FAITH 
platform, eliminating the third-party integration dependency and associated licensing 
costs. 
 
This Project Charter formally authorises the FAITH HRMS Probation Digitalisation initiative 
undertaken by Daythree Business Services Sdn. Bhd. It covers two parallel workflows: New-
Hire Probation (WF1) and Acting-Role Probation (WF2). 
 
2. Project Background & Business Case 
2.1 Background  
 
The organisation currently manages employee probation through a combination of manual 
tracking, email-based notifications, and physical or standalone digital letter preparation. 
This results in: 
 
• Inconsistent probation monitoring across departments and line managers 
• Missed review timelines due to lack of automated reminders 
• Delayed letter generation and signature collection 
• Inaccurate or late employment status updates in FAITH 
• Limited visibility for HR and management on probation pipeline status

3. Project Objectives 
• Deliver a fully functional Probation module within FAITH that automates the end-to-
end probation workflow as defined in Process V2.0 
• Implement grade-differentiated automated daily reminder notifications for all key 
milestones (Day 31, Day 61, Day 91, etc.) and throughout DR acceptance and 
signing windows
 
• Configure 7-day DR auto-acceptance with daily reminders for all monthly review 
cycles 
• Enforce HRBP 5-day SLA for letter generation from the point of HRBP notification 
• Enable digital letter generation (Confirmation, Extension, Non-Confirmation) with 
an internal e-signature and acknowledgement solution built within FAITH 
• Configure role-based access and task queues for LM, DR, HRBP, and PM roles 
• Ensure real-time synchronisation of employment status in FAITH upon probation 
outcome 
• Support the 6-month probation rule for M9-grade and above employees, with 
allowance for early confirmation 
• Provide HRBP with full pipeline visibility, SLA tracking, report generation and audit 
export capability 
• Deliver a Reporting & Analytics module covering 5 report types with on-screen, 
Excel/CSV export

6. Compliance & Audit 
To ensure the security and non-repudiation of the process, the following compliance 
requirements are mandatory for the internal acknowledgement module: 
x Digital Fingerprint: Every acknowledgement must capture the User ID, Date/Time 
Stamp, and the IP Address of the user. 
x Version Control: The system must store the exact version of the letter (PDF) that was 
presented to and acknowledged by the employee. 
x Immutable Logs: Audit logs must be stored in a read-only format to prevent tampering 
or manual alterations. 
x Reporting: The system must provide HRBPs with the ability to export acknowledgement 
reports for legal or other verification purposes.

9. Key Business Rules (Flow v2.0) 
Auto-Acceptance: Reviews for Month 1, 2, and 3 (for all grades) and Month 4, 5, and 6 (for 
M9+ grades) will auto-accept after 7 days if action is not taken. Daily reminders apply. 
M9+ Roles: Default probation period of 6 months with early confirmation eligibility. 
HRBP SLA: Letters must be generated within 3 business days of notification.
