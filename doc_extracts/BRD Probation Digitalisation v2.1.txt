BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT 
Probation Process Digitalisation 
1. Introduction 
1.1 Purpose & Scope  
This BRD defines the complete business, functional, and non-functional requirements for the 
Probation Process Digitalisation project on FAITH HRMS. It translates the agreed Probation Process 
V2.0 flow into structured user stories and acceptance criteria that will drive system configuration, 
development, and testing. 
Crucial Architectural Note: As of May 2026, the project will use an internal e-signature and 
acknowledgement solution built within FAITH. DocuSign integration has been removed from scope. All 
user stories, acceptance criteria, and dependencies have been updated to reflect this decision.  
 
1.2 Business Problem Statement 
• Inconsistent probation monitoring across departments and line managers 
• Missed review timelines due to lack of automated reminders 
• Delayed letter generation and signature collection 
• Inaccurate or late employment status updates in FAITH 
• Limited visibility for HR and management on probation pipeline status

2. Business Requirements  
• BR-REQ-01: System shall automatically create a probation record on employee creation 
and notify Payroll Manager (N-09). 
• BR-REQ-02: Line Manager shall initiate probation via the system, selecting probation type 
(New-Hire or Acting-Role). 
• BR-REQ-03: Probation duration and review cycle count shall be determined by grade and 
WF type — E08 AND BELOW WF1: 3 months/3 cycles; M09–M12 WF1: 6 months/6 cycles; 
all grades WF2: 6 months/3 cycles (BR-01/02/19). 
• BR-REQ-04: Acting-role probation shall require HOD approval before the probation record 
is activated. 
• BR-REQ-05: Line Managers shall set KPIs before the first review cycle commences. KPI 
targets visible to Direct Report. 
• BR-REQ-06: Month 1 review triggered on Day 31 for all grades (BR-03). 
• BR-REQ-07: Month 2 review auto-triggered on Day 61 for all grades (BR-04). 
• BR-REQ-08: E08 AND BELOW: Month 3 triggered after Month 2 DR acceptance. M09–M12: 
Months 3–6 each triggered after prior month DR acceptance (BR-05, BR-05a–05c). 
• BR-REQ-09: Daily reminders sent to DR during every active review acceptance window 
across all cycles (BR-06/07/08). 
• BR-REQ-10: 7-day auto-accept applies to all cycles for both grades (A-02). 
• BR-REQ-11: Draft review submissions do not advance workflow status (BR-10). 
• BR-REQ-12: Outcome selection for E08 AND BELOW is triggered after Month 3 DR 
acceptance. For M09–M12, outcome selection is triggered after Month 6 DR acceptance 
(BR-05c). 
• BR-REQ-13: Day 91 auto status change applies to E08 AND BELOW extension cycles only. It 
does not apply to M09–M12 (BR-09).

4.1 Probation initiation  
US-01: Line Manager View 
As a... Line Manager 
I want to... log in to FAITH, navigate to My Team Probation and view a list of my direct 
reports 
So that... I can verify the list is accurate and take action to initiate probation 
Priority Must Have 
Owner LM / FAITH IT 
Acceptance Criteria: 
x LM can access the Probation module and My Team Probation screen without error. 
x All active direct reports assigned to the LM are listed with name, employee ID, joined date, and 
probation status. 
x An escalation button is available to notify HRBP if the list is inaccurate. 
 
US-02: KPI Setting 
As a... Line Manager 
I want to... initiate probation for each direct report and set their KPIs and targets 
within FAITH 
So that... the system captures agreed performance expectations from day one 
Priority Must Have 
Owner LM / HRBP 
Acceptance Criteria: 
x LM can initiate probation and a KPI/Target form is presented. 
x KPI and Target fields are mandatory before submission. 
x On submission, status changes to Prob-KPI Review with workflow owner as LM. 
x If saved as draft, system retains draft status.
